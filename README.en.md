## What Is This?

Greed Island is a **text adventure game that runs entirely within Claude Code**. No installation steps, no external dependencies, no programming language required. All you need is `git` and [Claude Code](https://claude.ai/code).

Claude acts as the Game Master (GM), narrating the entire world through immersive text. Explore the island, collect cards, train your Nen abilities, make allies or enemies — every choice has consequences.

---

## Features

### A Faithful Recreation of Greed Island

Cities, wastelands, harbors, casinos — the locations from the original manga are all here. Cards are scattered throughout the world, each with unique acquisition conditions. Some require combat, some require wit, some demand a price you won't expect.

### Complete Nen System

The Water Divination test determines your Nen type. As you venture deeper, you'll unlock increasingly powerful Nen techniques and design your own unique Hatsu ability. The heavier the Vow, the greater the power — but so is the cost.

### Roguelike: Death Is Permanent

No save loading. No do-overs. The moment your HP hits zero, it's over.

But over doesn't mean wasted.

### This Island Has a Will of Its Own

You think you can change everything? Try.

### They're Alive

The characters on this island remember you. Every action you take changes how they see you.

## Basic Commands

| Command | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| `/start` | Begin your adventure |
| `/look` | Observe your surroundings |
| `/go [location]` | Travel somewhere |
| `/talk [character]` | Speak with someone |
| `/book` | Open your spell book |

You'll learn more commands naturally as you play. You can also say **Book** or **Gain** directly — the GM treats them as in-game spells.
