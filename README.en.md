## What Is This?

Greed Island is a **text adventure game that runs entirely within Claude Code**. No installation steps, no external dependencies, no programming language required. All you need is `git` and [Claude Code](https://claude.ai/code).

Claude acts as the Game Master (GM), narrating the entire world through immersive text. You explore the island, collect cards, train your Nen abilities, make allies or enemies — every choice you make shapes the island's fate.

---

## Features

### A Faithful Recreation of Greed Island

100 Specified Slot cards, 40 Spell cards, 12+ cities and regions. From the monthly contests in Antokiba to the dodgeball finals in Soufrabi, from the spell card shop in Masadora to the deadly gambles in Dorias — everything from the original manga is here waiting for you.

### Complete Nen System

The Water Divination test determines your Nen type. Ten, Zetsu, Ren, Hatsu, Gyo, Shu, En, Ken, Ko, In, Ryu — eleven Nen techniques unlock progressively as you level up. Design your own unique Hatsu ability, balancing your Vow against its power.

### Roguelike: Death Is Permanent

No save loading. No do-overs. The moment your HP hits zero, that run is over. But death isn't the end — each clear (or death) unlocks new knowledge, opening entirely different paths in your next run.

### This Island Has a Will of Its Own

You think you can change everything? Try.

### NPCs Remember You

Biscuit remembers every training session, her attitude softening with your effort. Hisoka watches from the shadows, his interest growing with your strength. Razor respects those who challenge him head-on. Genthru... is assessing whether you're worth a preemptive strike.

These memories persist across conversations and sessions. They're alive.

### Multi-Run: Every Playthrough Is a New Story

Clear the game and bring out 3 cards — next run, you start with full power and your heirloom cards at 100% effect. The tragedies you witnessed, the chances you missed, the choices you couldn't make in time — next run, you'll know what to do.

Every clear unlocks new possibilities. Some paths can only be seen by those who've known failure.

## Commands

| Command | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| `/start` | Start a new game or continue from where you left off |
| `/book` | Open your spell book — view all card collection progress |
| `/look` | Examine your current surroundings |
| `/go [location]` | Travel to another location |
| `/talk [NPC]` | Speak with a character |
| `/gain` | Convert a defeated enemy or discovered item into a card |
| `/cards` | Quick check of your card counts |
| `/use [card]` | Activate a card's effect |
| `/transform` | Convert a card back into a physical item |
| `/slotin [card]` | Place a card into its specified slot |
| `/clone [card] [ally]` | Duplicate a card for a teammate |
| `/memcard` | View your memory card — clear history, heirlooms, locked endings |

You can also say **Book** or **Gain** directly — the GM treats them as in-game spells.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I play without knowing Hunter × Hunter?**
A: Yes. The GM naturally explains all concepts during gameplay. No prior knowledge needed.

**Q: How long is one playthrough?**
A: Depends on your exploration depth. Rushing the main story takes a few hours; full exploration takes longer.

**Q: Is it really permanent death? No loading saves?**
A: Really. But your knowledge and heirloom cards carry over to the next run. Every death makes you stronger.
